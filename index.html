Gemini

Dates: May 21st - June 20th.
If you were born within three days of a sign change you may belong to a cusp of two zodiac signs (Gemini – Cancer cusp or Taurus – Gemini cusp). Element: Air Sign
Quality: Mutable
House: Third
Ruling planet: Mercury (Travel and all forms of communication)
Colors: Yellow (Gemini colors)
Tarot cards: The Lovers and Knight of Swords
Horoscope and lucky numbers: See Horoscope for Gemini and lucky numbers

Libra

Dates: September 23rd - October 22nd.
If you were born within three days of a sign change you may belong to a cusp of two zodiac signs (Virgo – Libra cusp or Libra – Scorpio cusp).
Element: Air
Quality: Cardinal
House: Seventh
Ruling planet: Venus
Colors: Indigo Blue (Libra colors)
Tarot cards: Justice and King of Swords
Horoscope and lucky numbers: See Horoscope for Libra and lucky numbers

Pisces

Dates: February 19th - March 20th.
If you were born within three days of a sign change you may belong to a cusp of two zodiac signs (Aquarius – Pisces cusp or Pisces – Aries cusp).
Element: Water Sign
Quality: Mutable
House: Twelfth
Ruling planet: Neptune (before Neptune’s discovery, Jupiter)
Colors: Green, Violet, or Turquoise
Tarot cards: The Moon and the Knight of Cups
Horoscope and lucky numbers: See Horoscope for Pisces and lucky numbers
